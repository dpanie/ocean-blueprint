How Much Do YOU Contribute to Ocean Acidification?

Choose one answer per question. Add up your points. Don’t rationalize. Pick what you actually do.

Quick reality check

This isn’t about guilt. It’s about honesty → then action. If you leave questions blank, you’re not “busy,” you’re dodging.

Transportation (biggest personal CO₂ driver)

1. How do you usually commute short distances (<3 miles)?

2. How many flights do you take per year?

3. What type of car do you mostly ride in?

Diet (direct CO₂ + indirect ocean impact)

4. How often do you eat red meat?

5. How often do you eat seafood?

6. How much food do you waste?

Energy & consumption

7. How is your home powered?

8. How often do you buy new clothes?

9. How long are your showers?

Waste & pollution

10. How consistently do you recycle correctly?

11. What do you do with chemicals (paint, cleaners, oil)?

12. How often do you use single-use plastics?

Awareness & action

13. How often do you think about ocean acidification?

14. Have you ever taken action to reduce your CO₂ footprint?
